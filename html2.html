Chocolate Soufflé

If you're looking for a dessert that's impressive enough for a dinner party but easy enough to make for a casual meal at home,this chocolate soufflé is it.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 8 ounces semisweet Chocolate
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3 large egg yolks, plus 4 large egg whites
  • ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly butter a baking dish
  2. In a large heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water, combine chocolate, vanilla, and 1/4 cup water.
  3. Stir until chocolate is melted and let it cool then add egg yolks
  4. In a large bowl beat egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form then add sugar
  5. In two additions, fold egg-white mixture into soufflé base
  6. Bake soufflé until puffed and set, 30 to 35 minutes.
